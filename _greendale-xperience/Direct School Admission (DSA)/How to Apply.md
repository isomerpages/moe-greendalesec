<h2>How to Apply</h2>
<p></p>
<p>The Direct School Admission (DSA) 2025 exercise starts <strong>on 7 May 2025 (11 a.m.) and ends on 3 June 2025 (3 p.m.)</strong>.</p>
<p>DSA-Sec 2025 exercise is now closed.</p>
