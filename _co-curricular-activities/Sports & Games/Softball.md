<p>The Greendale Softballers undergo structured and rigorous physical conditioning
and skills training that align with the high standards set by Coach Marcus
and his team of coaches. Through regular training sessions, students develop
resilience, discipline, teamwork, and a strong sense of sportsmanship.</p>

<p>In 2024, Greendale Secondary School hosted the <strong>Inter-School Softball Carnival</strong>,
where both the Girls’ and Boys’ Teams achieved commendable <strong>Second Place</strong> finishes.
At the <strong>National School Games (NSG) 2025</strong>, the Girls’ and
Boys’ Teams competed in <strong>League 2 (C Division)</strong>, demonstrating
determination and commitment as they represented the school with pride.</p>
<p>Our Softballers remain dedicated to continuous improvement, adapting to
the evolving demands of the sport while striving to perform to the best
of their abilities both on and off the field.</p>
