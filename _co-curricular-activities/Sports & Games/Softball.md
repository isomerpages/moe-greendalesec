<p>The Greendale Softballers undergo structured and rigorous physical conditioning
and skills training, guided by the high standards set by Coach Marcus and
his dedicated team of coaches. Through regular and purposeful training
sessions, students build resilience, discipline, teamwork, and a strong
sense of sportsmanship.</p>

<p>In 2024, Greendale Secondary School proudly hosted the Inter-School Softball
Carnival, where both the Girls’ and Boys’ Teams delivered outstanding performances
to clinch <strong>Second Place</strong> in their respective categories. Building
on this success, Greendale continued to make its mark at the <strong>National School Games (NSG) 2025</strong>,
with both the <strong>B Division and C Division teams</strong> taking part
in the competition. The Girls’ and Boys’ Teams competed in <strong>League 2 (C Division)</strong>,
while the <strong>B Division teams</strong> also represented the school with
determination and pride. Their perseverance and commitment on the field
reflected the strong sporting values of Greendale.</p>
