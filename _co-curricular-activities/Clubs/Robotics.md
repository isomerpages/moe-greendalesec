<h2><strong>Robotics Club: Engineering the Future</strong></h2>
<p>At GDLS Robotics, we are more than just a "coding club‚Äù. We are an incubator
for the next generation of engineers, designers, and innovators. We empower
students to transform abstract ideas into tangible solutions for real-world
challenges.</p>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Our Core Pillars</strong></h4>
<p>Our 4-year program is developed to move students from foundational skills
to innovation:</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; i.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Design &amp; Fabrication: Applying industrial-grade CAD software (e.g.,
SolidWorks and Autodesk Fusion 360) to design custom parts, brought to
life through 3D Printing and Laser Cutting.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ii.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Coding &amp; Intelligence: Moving beyond basic logic to explore Artificial
Intelligence (AI) and Micro-electronics (using Arduino and Anniken Andee
for app-controlled robotics).</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; iii.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Agile Problem Solving: Utilizing the Design Thinking framework to prototype
solutions for Singapore-relevant issues like sustainability and smart urban
living.</p>
<h4>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Character &amp; Future-Readiness.</strong></h4>
<p>We nurture the 21st Century Competencies essential for the future workforce:</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; i.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Resilience: Debugging complex code and troubleshooting mechanical failures
teaches a "never give up" attitude.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ii.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Collaboration: Most projects require team synergy, role specialization,
and effective communication.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; iii.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Ethics in Tech: We cultivate an understanding of the responsible use of
AI and technology in modern society.</p>
